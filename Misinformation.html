MISINFORMATION



Definition

The definition of misinformation is false or misleading information. The word “misinformation” mainly refers to the information itself, not describing an action. For example: Person A received information, and he told the information to person B. However, during the process of A giving out the information to B, A unconsciously said something wrong about the information. This makes the information from now on become misinformation. However, we don’t consider the action by A to be defined as disinformation. Since he is not intended to give out the wrong information to B.


Example

In 2011, Asia TV reported that Jiang Zemin (1926-2022) died because of heart issues. However, Jiang Zemin was still alive at that time. “Jiang Zemin died because of heart issues.” is a misinformation.
