About Us


Our firm was founded at the beginning of 2019 and we wish to provide trustworthy information to help people to distinguish between true and fake information. Our goal as a group of three is to spread truth via the internet to the wider community.

We know how important it is to have correct information in the current digital environment. Upholding the truth and honesty is what we whish. Our mission is to dispel false information by giving information on how we can know if the information is fake or not.

The foundation of our group is education. We offer useful guides, such as examples, videos, and external website, to improve media literacy and enable users to confidently navigate through the internet.

The values of our groups are independence, responsibility, and transparency. We also values our users and will adapt the constructive critisism from our users to build a better website, and more importantly, a kind and harmonious community.

Please come along with us in this era of internet, lets explore the wonderful internet with more confidence and the ability to distinguish what to believe and what not to believe. Also, check our website frequently for more new and lastest update on the information and education materials.
